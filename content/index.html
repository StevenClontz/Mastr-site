MasterIt

Freely available mathematics exercises

For students: use the exercises available in the navigation bar as practice.

For instructors: use these exercises in written assessments. Math notation may be extracted from the webpage by right-clicking formulas. For help using the Mastr platform programmatically (including generating exercises not on this site), contact the author Steven Clontz.

How to add this site as an app on your phone

On Android/Chrome: tap the browser menu, then tap "Add to Home screen".

On iOS/Safari: tap the share arrow, then tap "Add to Home Screen".
