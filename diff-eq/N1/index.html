N1 - Existence and uniqueness. Apply an existence and uniqueness theorem to an IVP. (ver. 1)

\[( t^{2} - 4 \, t )y'= {\left(t^{2} + 6 \, t + 5\right)} y - 2 \, t\hspace{2em}y( 7 )= -4\]
