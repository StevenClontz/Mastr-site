V4 - Subspaces

Example 1

V4 - Subspaces (ver. 1)

Consider the following two sets of Euclidean vectors:

\[U=\left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} x \\ y \\z \\ w \\ \end{array}\right] \,\middle|\,6x+1y=1z-2w \right\} \hspace{2em} W=\left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c} x \\ y \\z \\ w \\ \end{array}\right] \,\middle|\,6x+y^2=4z+2w \right\}.\]

Explain why one of these sets is a subspace of \(\mathbb{R}^4\), and why the other is not.

Answer.

\(U\) is a subspace of \(\mathbb{R}^4\) and \(W\) is not.
