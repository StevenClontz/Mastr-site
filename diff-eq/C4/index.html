C4 - Homogeneous second-order linear ODE (ver. 1)

Find the general solution to the given ODE.

\[y''-10y'+25y = 0\]

Answer.

\[y= k_{1} t e^{\left(5 \, t\right)} + k_{2} e^{\left(5 \, t\right)}\]
