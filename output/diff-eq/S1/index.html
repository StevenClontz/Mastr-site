S1: System of IVPs

Example 1

S1: System of IVPs (ver. 1)

Find the solution to the given system of IVPs.

\[x'= 2 \, x + 12 \, y\hspace{2em}x(0)=1\]

\[y'= 3 \, x - 3 \, y\hspace{2em}y(0)=-4\]

Answer.

\[x= -3 \, e^{\left(6 \, t\right)} + 4 \, e^{\left(-7 \, t\right)}\]

\[y= -e^{\left(6 \, t\right)} - 3 \, e^{\left(-7 \, t\right)}\]
